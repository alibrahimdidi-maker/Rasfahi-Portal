How to Use the Slideshow

The Zaad Academy Event Slideshow module is designed to easily browse through event highlights. Below are the instructions on how to use it.

1. Changing Slides:
Slides will transition automatically. However, to navigate manually, use the or arrows located on either side of the screen.

2. Pausing the Slideshow:
Hovering your mouse cursor over any image will pause the automatic rotation. Moving the mouse away will resume the slideshow.

3. Full Screen Mode:
To view an image in full screen or larger detail, simply double-click on the center of the image.

* For any technical issues regarding the display, please contact our support team.
